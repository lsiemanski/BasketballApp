Description

Player starts with the shot from left corner, then goes for the rebound and sprints with the ball to the right corner and shoots from there. Process repeats with spots 3, 4 and 5. After that player starts this cycle again from the left corner.

Points of emphasis

  • Don't focus on getting shot off quickly.
  • Containing good shot mechanics is more important than making a shot.

Time to start

Select number of shots you want to take from every spot and go!
