Description

This drill will improve shooting form and develop good shooting habits. Focus on proper mechanics of every single shot and be consistent. Start close to the basket, then make progressions and go further away from the basket. If you can't keep proper mechanics take shots from closer distance.

Points of emphasis

  • Feet - shoulder width apart.
  • Feet - slightly staggered and/or turned.
  • Strong and stable position.
  • L with arm - start from this position.
  • Up and Out - push up and out through your fingers.
